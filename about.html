A Little About Me

After many years as a small business owner in the UK, and as a hairstylist in New York and more recently in Sydney, I decided it was time to mix things up a little.
Wanting to challenge myself by doing something completely different, I decided that I would side-step into the tech arena.
After discovering UX design, a field that I believe is so important to any business' success, I chose to enrol at General Assembly to study their UX Design Immersive Course.
Being passionate about continued learning and wanting to further challenge myself I re-enrolled onto their Front End Web Development Course.

What really excites me about UX Design is that it allows me to draw upon my past experience in hairdressing of being customer-focused, my strong ability to communicate and actively listen and also to think creatively and strategically.
What I really enjoy about Front End Web Development is how even a few lines of code can produce magic before my eyes.
