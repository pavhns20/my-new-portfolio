Up The Road

Upcycling fashion ecommerce store built with WordPress.

About the project

The brief for this project was to build a website using WordPress for an upcycling fashion business that upcycles vintage garments, and also offers a custom tailoring service.
The request was for a dark, high-end feeling colour palette but it also needed to have a nod to their upcycling message.
It needed full shop functionality, with item selection through to cart and checkout. It also needed to have a blog and contact page.
