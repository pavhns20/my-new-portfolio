Luxury Access Club

Research, validation and design of a mobile website.

About the project

Research and validation of the potential new Luxury Access Club website that would enable users to experience luxury hotels and resorts without having to be an in-house guest.

Design of a mobile website with a tailored obective form the client, offering different membership options.

Giving a face to our data

From our research we identified 3 key personas- those wanting membership, single day-pass users & a supply side hotelier. Using our data it was time to put a face to our findings.

Facing the problem

Demand side problem statement

Our users need a way to access luxury hotels & resorts without having to book a room, so that they have the opportunity to experience luxury without the expense.

Supply side problem statement

Hotels need a way to occupy lesser used common areas in low season dips, which will increase revenue, maintaining the exclusivity & security of guests & staff.

Final design

Overall, the 10 users that we tested with were able to complete the tasks with minor issues. After testing with both demand side and industry experts, we arrived at our final design.

Day pass option

A CTA was added on the page of the chosen hotel giving users the option to purchase a single day pass or become a member.

Noticing that most users didn't know the difference between the two options, and could possibly give up on the purchase, we added a "Find Out More" CTA to the home page for clearer explanation.

Membership option

Here we demonstrate what the user's flow would be like when registering for membership and purchasing it.

Client feedback

The brief from the client was to research and validate the viability of Luxury Access Club as a potential new venture.
At the beginning of this project we were told that "design would be considered a plus", but we were determined to make that happen. On presenting the hi-fi prototype, the client was impressed by what we were able to deliver within the designated timeframe.

Thank you for all that you've put together with this project. Also thank you for your professionalism with everyone that you interviewed on behalf of Luxury Access Club- everyone that I've spoken to were really happy to work with you & your chat.

Key learnings

Although a hi-fi fidelity prototype wasn't one of the requested deliverables, when we tested with SME's, it seemed that they really appreciated having a tangible product to interact with.
I felt that by offering them the prototype to interact with they could actually understand how the Luxury Access Club website would work & this might not have been quite so clear when running usability testing with them if we only had a lo-fi prototype to offer them.
