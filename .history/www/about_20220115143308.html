Who am I?

So this is that awkward self-promotion section, huh?
Ok, I'll give it a whirl...

Fun fact- I used to be a hairdresser who has worked on Broadway, on some movies and even for a few advertising campaigns. My adult working life has pretty much been a been spent in hair salons across the globe from UK, to New York and more recently, Sydney. I also ran my own successful hairdressing business for many years in the UK (which is where I am from originally).


You know, hairdressing shares many similarities with UX design. Being able to read people to understand and be empathic to their needs and frustrations, interpreting that to who they are and gaining their trust quickly, is definitley beneficial in both.

They also both call for excellent collaboration skills and communication skills.
(You see the connection more now, right?).


As a UX Designer and more recently, Web Developer , I love being able to make things a little easier for people by turning problems into beautiful intuitive designs.

Creating user-friendly products and beautiful websites really makes me happy.


My most recent project was hand-coding this portfolio. It probably would have been easier to use WordPress to build it but I wanted to do it to prove to myself that I could- I like a challenge!.


Outside of work, I'm likely to be found cooking or eating, in the gym, or whenever possible, exploring through travel.
